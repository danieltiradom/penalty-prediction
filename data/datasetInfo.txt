FEATURES:

Kicker_Foot: Foot with which the kick was taken. 
Kicker_Side: Where did the kicker shoot.   ### VARIABLE OBJETIVO###
Home_goals: Amount of goals for the home team
Away_Goals: Amount of goals for the away team
Minute: Minute the shot was taken
Team_Type: If "H" the kicker team was Home, if "A" the kicker team was away.
